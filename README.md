<h1>Renewable_Rampage</h1>
<h2>Introduction:</h2>

<p>The product we aim to deliver is an interactive game that is inspired by the United Nations
sustainable development goals, particularly SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy. It will be a
simple clicker game.</p>

<h2>The Problem:</h2>
<p>Fossil fuels are still a major global energy source, contributing to climate change and
environmental damage. Many people are aware of renewable energy, but there is limited
engagement or understanding of how moving away from fossil fuels can happen at a large
scale.</p>

<h2>The Proposal:</h2>

<p>Develop a clicker-style incremental game where players invest in renewable energy sources
including solar, wind, and hydro power. Players gradually earn energy profits and use them to
buy fossil fuel companies and convert them into renewable energy producers, promoting
awareness of sustainable energy transitions.</p>
